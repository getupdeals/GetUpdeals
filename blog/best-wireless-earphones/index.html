📋 Buying Guide: What to Look For

Before you buy, consider these key features:

Battery Life

Look for at least 8-10 hours on a single charge. Many budget earphones offer 15-20 hours with the charging case.

Sound Quality

Check for balanced sound with good bass. Brands like boAt and OnePlus offer custom EQ settings.

Bluetooth Version

Bluetooth 5.0 or higher ensures stable connection and lower latency. Bluetooth 5.2 is even better.

Water Resistance

IPX4 or IPX5 is ideal for workouts and light rain. IPX7 is waterproof for heavy sweat.

🔊 Neckband vs True Wireless: Which One to Choose?

Both have pros and cons. Here's how to decide:

Neckband

Pros: Longer battery life, harder to lose, often cheaper. Cons: Wire around neck, slightly heavier.

True Wireless

Pros: No wires, compact case, modern look. Cons: Smaller battery, easier to misplace.

Pro Tip

If you use earphones for long hours (calls, travel), neckbands are more comfortable. For gym and casual use, true wireless are great.

🧼 Earphone Care & Maintenance

Proper care extends the life of your earphones:

Clean Regularly

Use a soft brush to remove earwax from the mesh. Wipe with a microfiber cloth.

Avoid Moisture

Even with IP rating, don't submerge. Sweat can damage over time.

Proper Charging

Don't leave charging overnight. Use the original cable to avoid battery damage.

Store Safely

Always store in the case when not in use to prevent loss and dust accumulation.
