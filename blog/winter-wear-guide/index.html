Fabric Care Guide:

1

Wool Care

Hand wash in cold water with wool detergent. Lay flat to dry. Never wring.

2

Acrylic Care

Machine wash cold, gentle cycle. Tumble dry low. Resists wrinkles.

3

Front-load machine only. Use tennis balls in dryer to fluff filling.
