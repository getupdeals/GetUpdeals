📋 Kitchen Appliance Buying Guide

Before you click "buy", consider these key factors:

Wattage & Power

Higher wattage usually means better performance. For mixer grinders, 500W+ is ideal; for air fryers, 1400W+ is good.

Build Quality

Look for stainless steel jars, ABS plastic body, and copper windings for durability. Check warranty (2+ years preferred).

Noise Level

Mixer grinders and blenders can be loud. Check reviews for noise levels if you have a sensitive household.

Brand & Service

Established brands like Preethi, Bajaj, Philips have wide service networks, ensuring easy repairs.

🧼 Appliance Care & Maintenance

Extend the life of your kitchen appliances:

Clean After Each Use

Wipe down exteriors, wash jars and accessories. Prevents residue buildup and odors.

Unplug When Not in Use

Saves electricity and protects against power surges.

Avoid Overloading

Don't exceed capacity. For mixers, give rest periods to prevent motor burnout.

Keep Dry

Store in dry places to prevent rust and electrical hazards.
