Amazon & Flipkart Sale Tips: How to Save Maximum

By Shopping Expert December 18, 2024 12 min read 0 comments

Master the art of shopping during mega sales

Why Sales Are Worth It

Amazon Great Indian Festival and Flipkart Big Billion Days are the biggest shopping events in India. With proper planning, you can save up to 60-70% on electronics, fashion, and home appliances. This guide compiles expert strategies to help you maximize savings.

Key Fact

Over 70% of products see their lowest price of the year during these sales. But you need to act fast – top deals sell out in hours.

📝 Pre-Sale Preparation (1-2 Weeks Before)

Create Wishlists

Add desired items to your Amazon/Flipkart wishlist. You'll get notified when prices drop.

Check Card Offers

Know which cards give maximum discount. Usually SBI, HDFC, ICICI offer 10% instant discount.

Update Apps

Ensure the latest version of Amazon/Flipkart apps is installed for early access notifications.

Track Prices

Use tools like Keepa or CamelCamelCamel to see price history and set alerts.

💰 Bank Offers & Card Discounts

Bank offers are the biggest chunk of savings. Here's how to leverage them:

  • SBI Credit Card: Typically 10% instant discount (up to ₹1500-₹2500). Often works on both Amazon and Flipkart.
  • HDFC Bank: 10% discount on debit/credit cards with minimum purchase.
  • ICICI Bank: 10% off on Amazon, sometimes on Flipkart.
  • Axis Bank: 5-10% cashback via GrabDeals or card offers.
  • OneCard & Others: Check specific sales for additional 5-10% cashback.

Pro Tip

If you don't have the right card, ask a friend or family member to pay. You can repay them instantly via UPI.

How to Combine Bank Offers with Coupons:

Most sales allow you to apply a coupon on top of the bank discount. So your final price = sale price - coupon value - bank discount.

🏷️ Coupon Stacking Tricks

You can often stack multiple coupons on Amazon and Flipkart:

Product-specific Coupons

Click "Apply Coupon" on product page – these are usually 5-15% off.

Store-wide Coupons

Sometimes the brand or seller offers an extra discount code.

Coins & SuperCoins

Flipkart SuperCoins can be used to get additional discount up to ₹500.

Browser Extensions

Use extensions like Honey or PaisaWapas to automatically find coupon codes at checkout.

Real Example

Product price ₹10,000 → Sale price ₹7,000 → Product coupon 10% = ₹6,300 → Bank offer 10% = ₹5,670. That's a total saving of 43%!

📊 Price Tracking & Alerts

Don't rely on "claimed" discounts. Check real price history:

  • Keepa (Amazon): Shows price history graph right on product page. Know if the current price is actually low.
  • CamelCamelCamel: Another price tracker for Amazon.
  • Buyhatke: Browser extension that compares prices across Amazon and Flipkart.
  • Price History Apps: Use "Price Tracker" apps to set alerts for when your desired product drops below a certain price.

Pro Tip

Often the best price is not on the first day of sale, but on the last day when sellers clear stock. Monitor for flash sales.

♻️ Exchange & Upgrade Programs

Both Amazon and Flipkart offer exchange discounts when you trade in old products. This can give you extra ₹2,000-₹10,000 off.

  • Mobile Phones: Exchange your old phone for up to ₹15,000 discount.
  • Laptops: Exchange old laptops for ₹5,000-₹20,000 off.
  • Appliances: Exchange old mixer, TV, fridge for discount.
  • Fashion: Some sellers offer "exchange old clothes" but limited.

Check Terms

Exchange value depends on the condition and age of the product. Use the "Exchange" filter to see applicable products.

Final Checklist for Sale Day

  • ✅ Wishlist ready
  • ✅ Bank cards ready (check limits)
  • ✅ Coupons collected
  • ✅ Price alerts set
  • ✅ Exchange product ready (if applicable)
  • ✅ Logged into all accounts

Follow these tips, and you'll get the best deals without stress. Bookmark this page and share it with friends who love to shop!
