üßº Sneaker Care & Maintenance

Proper care extends your sneakers' life significantly:

Regular Cleaning

Brush off dirt after each use. Deep clean every 2 weeks.

Water Protection

Use waterproof spray on canvas/leather sneakers.

Proper Drying

Air dry in shade. Never use heater/dryer.

Rotation

Rotate between 2-3 pairs. Extends life of each.

Cleaning by Material:

Canvas Sneakers

Mix mild detergent + water. Soft brush. Air dry.

Leather Sneakers

Damp cloth + leather cleaner. Condition monthly.

Mesh Sneakers

Remove laces. Soft brush + mild soap. Air dry.

Deodorizing Tip

Baking soda overnight removes odors. Tea bags also work well.
