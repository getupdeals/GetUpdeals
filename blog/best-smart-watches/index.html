📋 Smartwatch Buying Guide

Before you buy, consider these key factors:

Display Quality

AMOLED > LCD. Brightness (500+ nits) for outdoor visibility. Always-on display is a plus.

Battery Life

Look for 5+ days with typical use. Fast charging (2 hours) is convenient.

Bluetooth Calling

Many budget watches now support calling via built-in speaker & mic – a must for convenience.

Health Sensors

Heart rate, SpO2, sleep tracking, stress monitoring. Accuracy varies – check reviews.

⚙️ Must-Have Features in a Budget Smartwatch

Water Resistance

IP68 or 5ATM rated for swimming and showering. Essential for active users.

Sports Modes

At least 100+ sports modes for accurate workout tracking. Multi-sport support is a bonus.

GPS

Built-in GPS is rare under ₹5000, but some models offer connected GPS (via phone).

Music Control

Control phone music, view notifications, and even store music on some watches.

Pro Tip

If you prioritize notifications and fitness tracking, go for a watch with a companion app that's well-reviewed (NoiseFit, boAt Wave, etc.).

🧼 Smartwatch Care & Maintenance

Extend the life of your smartwatch:

Clean Regularly

Wipe with a soft cloth after workouts to prevent sweat corrosion.

Avoid Overcharging

Unplug once full. Don't leave charging overnight to preserve battery health.

Store Safely

Keep away from magnets and extreme temperatures. Use screen protector if possible.

Update Firmware

Regular updates fix bugs and add new features. Keep the companion app updated.
