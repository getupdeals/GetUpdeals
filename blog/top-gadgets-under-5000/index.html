🛒 Smart Buying Guide: What to Look For

Not all gadgets under ₹5K are equal. Here's what matters most:

Battery Life

For TWS earbuds: look for 30+ hours total with case. Smartwatches: 7+ days in basic mode.

Build & Durability

IPX4/IP67 rating for sweat/rain resistance is a must for wearables and audio gear.

Chip & Connectivity

Bluetooth 5.2+ ensures stable connection. Fast charging support saves time.

Warranty & Support

Brands like boAt, Noise, OnePlus offer 1-year warranty. Check service centers.

📱 Categories: Which Gadget Suits You?

Wireless Earbuds

Best for music, calls & gym. Look for low latency gaming mode and ENC.

Smartwatches

Track steps, SpO2, heart rate. Great for fitness enthusiasts and daily notifications.

Power Banks

20,000mAh with fast charging is ideal for travelers. Multiple ports preferred.

Routers & Accessories

Dual-band routers, fast chargers, and smart plugs under 5K boost connectivity.

🧴 Tech Care Tips: Prolong Device Life

Optimal Charging

Avoid overnight charging; keep battery between 20-80% for longevity.

Keep Dry

Even IP-rated gadgets shouldn't be submerged. Wipe sweat/moisture after use.

Software Updates

Keep firmware updated for better performance and security patches.

Clean Ear Tips

Regularly clean earbud meshes to maintain sound quality and hygiene.

Pro Tip

Use a screen protector for smartwatches if you have an active lifestyle. Scratches reduce resale value.
