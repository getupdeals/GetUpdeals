🧵 Denim Fabric Guide

Understanding denim types helps you make better choices:

100% Cotton

Durable and breathable. Softens with wear. Best for straight and relaxed fits.

Cotton + Elastane (Stretch)

98-99% cotton + 1-2% elastane. Provides comfort and shape retention.

Wash Types

Dark wash: formal, slimming. Light wash: casual, distressed look.

Denim Weight

10-14 oz is ideal for Indian climate. Heavier for winter, lighter for summer.

📏 Jeans Fit Guide: Find Your Perfect Match

Choosing the right fit is crucial for comfort and style. Here's a breakdown of fits available under ₹999:

Slim Fit

Modern, tailored silhouette. Best for lean or athletic builds. Creates a sharp look.

Regular Fit

Classic cut with room for movement. Suits most body types. Ideal for casual and office wear.

Straight Fit

Uniform width from thigh to ankle. Timeless style, works for all body types.

Relaxed Fit

Loose and comfortable. Best for casual wear and relaxed styles.

Rise Types:

  • Low Rise: Sits below waist, trendy look.
  • Mid Rise: Sits at natural waist, versatile.
  • High Rise: Above waist, flattering and comfortable.

Pro Tip

For office wear, choose dark wash regular or slim fit. For casual outings, experiment with light wash and distressed styles.

🧺 Jeans Care & Maintenance

Proper care extends your jeans' life significantly:

Wash Less Frequently

Wash every 4-5 wears. Spot clean minor stains.

No Fabric Softener

Breaks down fibers. Use vinegar instead for softness.

Inside Out Wash

Protects color and reduces fading.

Air Dry Only

Dryer damages elastic fibers and causes shrinkage.

Breaking In New Jeans

Wear them at home for a few hours before first wash. The fabric will mold to your body shape.
