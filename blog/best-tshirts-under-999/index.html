üß∫ Care & Maintenance Tips

Proper care extends your t-shirt's life significantly:

Wash in Cold Water

Use mild detergent and cold water to prevent fading.

Avoid Fabric Softeners

They break down cotton fibers over time.

Air Dry When Possible

Direct sunlight can fade colors. Dry in shade.

Iron Inside Out

Prevents direct heat on printed designs.

Important:

Never use bleach on colored t-shirts. Wash dark colors separately for first 3-4 washes.
