🧵 T-Shirt Fabric Guide

Understanding fabrics helps you choose the right t‑shirt for comfort and durability:

100% Cotton

Breathable, soft, and hypoallergenic. Best for everyday wear. May shrink slightly.

Cotton Blends (Polyester/Cotton)

Wrinkle-resistant, durable, and holds shape better. Ideal for travel and active wear.

Tri‑blend (Cotton/Poly/Rayon)

Super soft, drapes well, and resists shrinking. Premium feel at affordable prices.

Jersey vs. Pique

Jersey: soft and stretchy (basic tees). Pique: textured, breathable (polos).

📏 T-Shirt Fit Guide: Find Your Perfect Style

Choosing the right fit enhances your look. Here's a breakdown of popular fits available under ₹999:

Regular Fit

Classic cut with room for movement. Suits most body types. Ideal for casual and semi‑formal wear.

Slim Fit

Modern, tailored silhouette. Best for lean or athletic builds. Creates a sharp look.

Oversized

Trendy streetwear style. Relaxed, comfortable. Pair with slim bottoms for balance.

Neck Styles:

  • Crew Neck: Classic round neck, versatile.
  • V‑Neck: Elongates the neck, adds sophistication.
  • Polo: Collared, smart‑casual.
  • Henley: Buttoned placket, rugged style.

Pro Tip

For office wear, choose solid colors in regular or slim fit. For casual outings, experiment with prints and oversized fits.

🧺 T-Shirt Care & Maintenance

Proper care extends your t‑shirt's life significantly:

Wash Inside Out

Protects prints and colors from fading.

Avoid High Heat

Hot water and dryers cause shrinkage and damage elastic fibers.

Air Dry

Lay flat or hang dry to maintain shape and fabric integrity.

Separate Colors

Wash dark and light colors separately to prevent bleeding.
