Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency is at the core of everything we do. Here's how affiliate links work on GetUpDeals.

Our Commitment to Transparency

GetUpDeals is a participant in various affiliate marketing programs, which means we may earn commissions when you click on certain links and make a purchase. This comes at no additional cost to you.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracked URLs that help us identify when a visitor is referred from our site to a partner store (like Amazon, Flipkart, Myntra, etc.). If you buy a product after clicking one of these links, we may receive a small commission – typically a percentage of the sale price.

Why Do We Use Affiliate Links?

Affiliate commissions help us cover the costs of running GetUpDeals – including hosting, content creation, and the time our team spends hunting for the best deals. This allows us to keep our service free for you, our readers.

Does It Affect Your Price?

Absolutely not. The price you pay is exactly the same as if you visited the store directly. In some cases, we even negotiate exclusive discounts for our audience, so you may actually save more by using our links.

How Do We Choose Which Products to Feature?

We only recommend products and deals that we genuinely believe offer good value. Our editorial content is independent, and we never let potential commissions influence our reviews or recommendations. If a deal is not worth your money, we won't feature it – regardless of commission.

Our promise: We will always clearly label affiliate links and remain transparent about our partnerships. If you ever have questions, feel free to contact us.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you get paid if I don't buy anything?

No, commissions are only earned when a purchase is completed through an affiliate link. If you click and leave without buying, we don't earn anything.

How can I identify an affiliate link on your site?

We often mark affiliate links with a small "Affiliate" badge or an asterisk. In blog posts, we include a disclosure at the top of the page. When in doubt, assume that outbound links to stores may be affiliate links.

Do you accept payment for positive reviews?

Never. We do not accept money or free products in exchange for positive coverage. All opinions are our own and based on genuine research.

What happens to my data when I click an affiliate link?

The partner store may set cookies to track the referral. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of the respective stores for details. We do not share any personal information with them – only a referral ID is passed.
