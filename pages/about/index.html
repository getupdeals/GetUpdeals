About GetUpDeals

We're on a mission to help you save money without compromising on quality.

Our Story

GetUpDeals was founded in 2020 by a group of passionate shoppers who were tired of missing out on the best online deals. We realized that with hundreds of e‑commerce stores launching sales every day, it was nearly impossible to track the best offers without spending hours browsing.

So we built a platform that does the heavy lifting for you. Our team of deal hunters scans thousands of products daily, verifies discounts, and brings you only the most worthwhile offers – from fashion and electronics to gadgets and home appliances.

Today, GetUpDeals is trusted by over 50,000 shoppers who have collectively saved more than ₹25 crore. And we're just getting started.
