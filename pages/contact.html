Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How can I submit a deal to GetUpDeals?
You can submit deals by emailing us at deals@getupdeals.com with the deal details, store link, discount information, and validity period. Our team reviews all submissions within 24-48 hours.
How long does it take to get a response?
We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For partnership and business inquiries, we typically respond within 48 hours.
Can I advertise on GetUpDeals?
Yes, we offer advertising opportunities for relevant brands and services. Please contact our partnerships team at partners@getupdeals.com for advertising rates and available placements.
Do you have an affiliate program?
Yes, we work with various affiliate networks including Cuelinks and Impact Radius. If you're a content creator or website owner, you can join our program to earn commissions by promoting our deals. Contact affiliates@getupdeals.com for more information.
How do I report a broken deal or incorrect information?
Please email us at support@getupdeals.com with the deal URL and details of the issue. We appreciate your help in keeping our platform accurate and up-to-date.
