Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about how we earn commissions through affiliate partnerships

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

Full Transparency

GetUpDeals participates in various affiliate marketing programs. This means we may earn commissions on qualifying purchases made through our links at no additional cost to you.

We believe in complete transparency with our users. This page explains how our affiliate partnerships work and how they support our platform.

Important Notice

Using our affiliate links does not increase the price you pay. In fact, we often help you find better deals and discounts. The commission we earn helps support our platform and allows us to continue providing free deal discovery services.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking links that allow us to earn a small commission when you make a purchase through them. Here's how they work:

You Click Our Link

When you click on a deal link on GetUpDeals, you're redirected to the retailer's website through our affiliate tracking link.

You Make a Purchase

If you decide to buy something during that session (usually within 24-48 hours), the retailer tracks that it came from our link.

We Earn a Commission

The retailer pays us a small commission for referring you. This comes from their marketing budget, not from your pocket.

You Pay Nothing Extra

The price you pay is exactly the same as if you went directly to the retailer. Our commission doesn't affect your price.

How Commissions Support Our Platform

The commissions we earn help us:

  • Maintain and improve our website and mobile apps

  • Keep our services free for all users

Without affiliate commissions, we wouldn't be able to provide the quality of service that our users expect and deserve.

Our Editorial Integrity

Deal Selection Process

We maintain strict editorial independence in our deal selection and content creation. Here's our process:

  1. Independent Research: Our team researches deals independently based on user value, not commission rates.
  2. User-First Approach: We prioritize deals that offer genuine savings and value to our users.
  3. Quality Over Commission: We never promote poor-quality products or bad deals just because they offer higher commissions.
  4. Regular Verification: All deals are verified for accuracy before being published.
  5. Transparent Ranking: When we rank or recommend products, we clearly disclose our criteria.

We believe that maintaining user trust is more valuable than any individual commission. That's why we're committed to providing honest, unbiased deal information regardless of affiliate relationships.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do affiliate links increase the price I pay?

No. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the retailer. The commission comes from the retailer's marketing budget.

Are all links on GetUpDeals affiliate links?

Most product and deal links are affiliate links, but not all. Some links are for informational purposes only. We clearly mark affiliate relationships where appropriate.

How can I support GetUpDeals without making purchases?

You can support us by sharing our content, following us on social media, signing up for our newsletter, and providing feedback. Every bit of engagement helps!

Do you get paid for positive reviews?

No. We do not accept payment for positive reviews. Our reviews are based on genuine research, user feedback, and product testing when possible.

What happens if I return a product bought through an affiliate link?

If you return a product, the commission is typically reversed. This is standard practice across all affiliate programs.
