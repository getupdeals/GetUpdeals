How to Choose the Right Refrigerator

  • Capacity: 150-250L for 2-4 members, 300L+ for larger families
  • Type: Single door (budget), Double door (most popular), Side-by-side (premium)
  • Technology: Inverter compressors save 30-40% electricity
  • Features: Frost-free, smart cooling, vegetable crispers
  • Star Rating: 3-star minimum, 5-star for maximum savings

Air Conditioner Buying Guide 2024

  • Ton Calculation: 150 sq.ft = 1 ton, add 0.5 ton for each floor above
  • Type: Window AC (budget), Split AC (efficient), Inverter AC (best savings)
  • ISEER Rating: Higher ISEER = better efficiency (aim for 4.5+)
  • Features: Copper condenser, anti-dust filter, sleep mode
